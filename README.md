🌟 ECHOBUDDY - AI Companion Box for Left-Behind Children

"Because every kid deserves someone who listens"

🎯 What is ECHOBUDDY?

ECHOBUDDY is a AI box that keeps lonely kids company when their parents aren't home.

You know those nights when your mum or dad is working far away, and you wish you had someone to talk to? That's exactly why we built this.

It's like a friendly robot that:

Listens to your feelings 🎧
Tells you cool stories 🌙
Helps with homework 📚
Teaches you how to stay safe 🛡️
And it works WITHOUT the internet - so it works even in places with bad WiFi!

✨ What Can It Do?

Feature	What it means
🤗 Emotional Support：	Having a bad day? ECHOBUDDY listens and makes you feel better
📚 Homework Help：	Stuck on a question? ECHOBUDDY explains things patiently
🌙 Bedtime Stories：	Can't sleep? ECHOBUDDY tells you awesome stories
🛡️ Safety Tips：	Learn about fire safety, stranger danger, water safety and more
🎮 Fun Games：	Word games, riddles, Chinese idioms - learning is fun!
👨‍👩 Two Characters：	Choose "AI Mum" or "AI Dad" - whoever feels more comfortable

💻 How It Works

[You Talk/Speak] → [Microphone] → [Speechrecognition] → [AI Brain] → [text to audio] → [You Hear]
                      🔇 OFFLINE! No WiFi needed 💪



Tech We Use

Voice Recognition: python
Voice Output: python
Runs on: Raspberry Pi
